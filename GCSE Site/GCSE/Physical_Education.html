P.E. Sport & Leisure

 
 
 
 

Learning Aims

 

The Physical Education Sport and Leisure course is a compulsory requirement for all students. The aims of the course are to provide opportunities for students to engage in a number of different activities that will help to engage their interests, supplement their current activities and provide opportunities for lifelong learning.

There are 4 aspects to the course:

  1. •Improving individual fitness

  2. •Understanding the human body and improving its performance

  3. •Increase participant into lifelong learning

  4. •Opportunity to compete
